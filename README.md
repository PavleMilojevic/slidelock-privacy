# Privacy Policy — SlideLock

Effective date: March 2, 2026

SlideLock (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Information we collect

- Account information: email address and authentication data required to create and access your account.
- Gameplay data: progress such as XP, tier, statistics and in-game settings linked to your account.

## How we use information

- To provide login and account access.
- To save your progress and show your statistics.
- To maintain and improve app performance and reliability.

## Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is stored and processed using our backend provider (Supabase) to enable account and progress features.

## Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including using secure connections where applicable.

## Children

SlideLock is not designed to collect personal data from children without parental consent.
